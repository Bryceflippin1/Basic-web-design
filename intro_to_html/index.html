Belle Fourche, South Dakota

© 2025 Welcome to Belle Fourche sign

At a population of just under 6,000 people, the seed of Butte County is a small town called Belle Fourche. It's probably a town you've never heard of, and you also might be thinking why write about a random small town. Well, it's the geographic center of the United States. It wasn't always the center though. Before we acquired Alaska and Hawaii the center of the nation was Lebanon, Kansas. The town now tracks tourism for its center of the nation monument and along with it the Tri-State Museum where you can stand in three states at once.

Why it's important to Me

It all started one random day. Me and some friends were managing a booth at an art show, and this guy came over to us and asked us if we had ever been to South Dakoata. I told him I had planned it once and that me and some friends were planning on going to the world's largest potluck at Sioux Falls, South Dakota. He said he'd been there and proceeded to tell us about a huge rodeo that does fireworks on July 4th every year and about how cool he thought Mount Rushmore was. Hours go by, he reapproaches with his wife, and she also says she is from South Dakota. She asked us what we thought the center of the nation was. My friend guessed Kanas, and she told us it was Belle Fourche. Now it's just been an inside joke between me and my friends.
